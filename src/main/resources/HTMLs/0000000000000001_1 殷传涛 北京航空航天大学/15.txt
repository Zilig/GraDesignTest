            <p class="atexp-abstract-ch" id="atexp-abstract" style="margin-top: 20px;"><span class="atexp-abstract-bold">摘要：</span>随着互联网的发展和普及，人们对复杂网络的研究越来越多。复杂网络中存在社区结构，而找出社区结构有助于挖掘复杂网络中一些有用的信息，因此社区划分成为了近年来的一个研究热点。目前常见的社区划分算法有基于图论的算法和基于层次聚类的方法，这些算法中，有些需要预先确定社区的数目，有些时间复杂度非常高。本文提出了一种基于层次聚类的社区划分算法，从全局中心节点建立初始社区，再根据联系紧密程度一层一层地扩展社区，最后把一些过小的社区融合到大社区中。另外，本文在模块度的基础上提出了扩展模块度的概念，可以更好的衡量社区划分结果的好坏。</p>

            	<p class="atexp-abstract-eh col999" style="margin-top: 20px;"><span class="atexp-abstract-bold">Abstract：</span>Due to Internet's development and popularization, there are more and more researches on complex networks. Researches show that there exists community structure in complex networks. Finding out community structure helps to extract useful information in complex networks, so research on community detection is a hotspot in recent years. Common algorithms for community detection include algorithms based on graph theory and algorithms based on hierarchical clustering. Some existing algorithms for community detection need to know the number of communities, while some have high time complexity. This paper proposed a method for community detection based on hierarchical clustering, starting from global central nodes to initialize communities, then expanding communities layer by layer according to link strength, and at last merge little communities into big communities. In addition, this paper proposed the definition of extensive modularity, which can better evaluate algorithms' quality.</p>
