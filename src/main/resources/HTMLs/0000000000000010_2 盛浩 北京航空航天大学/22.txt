<h2>个人简历</h2><p>
<p>My research mainly focuses on advanced structrual alloys, such as ultra-high strength bearing/gear steels and high entropy alloys. I am interested in modelling the evolution of microstructures and mechanical properties of these alloys upon plastic deformation. I also work on the design and modelling of high entropy alloys for various application scenarios by means of machine learning.</p><p><br /></p><p>My research style is to establish mathematical models to correlate macro-scale phenomenology with micro-scale interactions and to reveal underlying mechanisms of these transitions,&nbsp;with experimental validations.&nbsp;</p><p><strong><br /></strong></p><p><strong>Selected Publications:</strong></p><p><strong><br /></strong></p><p>[1] Int.
